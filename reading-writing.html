Tamil reading and writing classes are designed for students who wish to develop a deeper understanding of the language. The course focuses on improving reading fluency, correct pronunciation, and strong writing skills. Students will learn Tamil grammar in detail, including sentence structure, vocabulary, and proper usage of words. Special attention is given to understanding classical and modern Tamil literature, helping learners appreciate the richness and depth of the language. Through regular practice and guided learning, students will gradually gain confidence in reading complex texts and expressing their thoughts clearly and correctly in written Tamil.
